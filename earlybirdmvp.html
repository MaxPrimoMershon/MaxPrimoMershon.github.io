Earlybird MVP

Project Goal: Create a minimal viable product that allows founders to navigate the process of developing a lean tech start-up with benchmarks to assess its viability.

Problem: Inexperienced and non-technical founders lack the knowledge to gauge the viability of their start-up concept and navigate the process of developing it.

Solution: Create a portal of assessments that allow founders to gauge the viability of their start-up concept and knowledge of the lean start-up process.

My Role:

  • Researched the lean start-up process and key attributes of successful tech start-ups
  • Researched competitors and identified gaps in the market
  • Whiteboarded multiple ideas for the MVP
  • Developed multiple iterations of a prototype for the MVP using HTML, CSS, jQuery, and Bootstrap
